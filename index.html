Exploring Australian Tourism Trends (2014-2024)

Analyzing tourist movement, reasons for visits, and state-based distribution across Australia.

Tourist Distribution by State (2014-2024)

This map shows the distribution of tourists across Australian states from 2014 to 2024, visualizing regional trends over time. Larger circles represent higher numbers of tourists in a state, offering insights for tourism boards and policymakers.

Grouped Bar Chart: Tourist Comparison by State and Year

This chart compares the number of tourists visiting various Australian states each year from 2014 to 2024. It provides valuable insights for businesses and local authorities to track state-wise trends and plan accordingly.

Heatmap: Reasons for Visiting Australia

This heatmap displays the reasons tourists visited Australia, categorized by their length of stay, between 2014 and 2024. It provides insights into visitor motivations, helping businesses and tourism stakeholders tailor their offerings.

Heatmap: State-Based Tourist Data

This heatmap shows the total number of tourists visiting each Australian state from 2014 to 2024. It helps policymakers and tourism boards identify tourism growth trends across regions.

Pie Chart: Movement Categories by Year

This pie chart visualizes the distribution of movement categories, such as short-term and long-term visitors, over the years from 2014 to 2024. The dropdown filter allows for an interactive view of how these categories changed over time.

Data Source: Australian Tourism Reports (2014-2024)
