Exploring Australian Tourism Trends

An interactive visualization of tourism data in Australia.

Tourist Arrivals by State

The map below shows the number of tourists in each Australian state. Darker colors indicate higher numbers of tourists. You can hover over a state on the map to see the specific tourist count.

International Tourists Over Time

The line chart below displays the changes in international tourist numbers in Australia from 2015 to 2022. By observing this chart, you can understand the development of the tourism industry and the impact of external factors (like global events) on tourist numbers.

Data Source: Australian Bureau of Statistics
